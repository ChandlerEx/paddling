Data Sources & Methodology

Station: NOAA 9414750 (Alameda) for wind and tides.

Local Chop: estimated from Alameda wind using height ≈ 0.10 × wind(knots), capped at ~3.5 ft. States: glassy (<6 kt), light (6–11), moderate (12–17), rough (18–24), very rough (25+).

Current Tide (Prediction): NOAA tide predictions (datum MLLW, LST/LDT). “Now” is a linear interpolation between the last and next Hi/Lo event; trend shows flooding (rising) or ebbing (falling).

Why no offshore buoy/current: conditions outside the Golden Gate differ substantially from inside the Bay; this dashboard prioritizes Alameda-local signals.
